# Mirror Vision Zine (2018)

A digital version of the "Mirror Vision Zine (2018)" by Jay Rosen.

**Publication Date:** July 2018

**Available on:** [Google Play Books](https://play.google.com/store/books/details/Jay_Rosen_Mirror_Vision?id=XDEPEQAAQBAJ)

<table>
  <tr>
    <td style="vertical-align: top; padding-right: 20px;">
      <p>This zine chronicles "Mirror Vision," a collection of experiential wearable glass art by Jay Rosen. The pieces have been featured in over 200 shows across North America, and the photographs in this volume were captured during four national tours with the synth-pop band TIME (2017-2018).</p>
      <p>"Mirror Vision" is designed to fundamentally alter perception. Each mask, often based on platonic solids, transforms the wearer's sight into an omnipresent, twisted, and kaleidoscopic vision of the world. The art lies in the interplay of transparency, opacity, and infinite reflection.</p>
      <p>Beyond the visual effect, a significant part of the experience is observing how individuals interact with each other and their environment while their perception is so uniquely filtered. This zine documents those moments of interaction, fashion, and altered reality.</p>
